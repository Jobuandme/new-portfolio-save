About Me

I’m Michael Gvozdek

I turn the world around me into vibrant, creative projects. Currently, I'm studying Global Business
and Digital Art at the University of Waterloo’s Stratford School of Interaction Design and Business.
Digital design has been my focus since highschool and I’ve picked up experience with Photoshop,
Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, and Premiere Pro. In combination with my design skills, I have
experience with coding creative projects in Python, Java, Javascript, and HTML/CSS.

My passion for BMX has built the foundation of my creative style, with the vibrant, bold, and
gritty nature of skateparks bleeding into my designs. The graffiti I see around me every day has
inspired me to pick up illustration as a hobby, which is reflected in many of my projects.
Time leading my high school yearbook team as well as directing my first documentary
has given me excellent project management and collaboration skills.
